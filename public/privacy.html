Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

When you join the HiveOut waitlist, we collect your email address and the time of signup. We do not collect any other personal data at this stage.

How we use it

Your email is used solely to notify you when HiveOut launches. We will not send marketing emails, sell your data, or share it with third parties.

Data storage

Waitlist data is stored in a secure Google Sheets spreadsheet accessible only to the HiveOut team.

Your rights

You can request removal from the waitlist at any time by emailing hiveoutofficial@gmail.com.
